Upload Users

Below is a full list of columns that NEED to be in the CSV

  • accountno A unique identifier for each user (should stay the same year to year)
  • name Full name of the participant
  • firm The company of the participant
  • title The job title of the participant
  • city The address city of the participant
  • state The address state of the participant
  • bio The biography of the participant
  • phone1 The phone number of the participant
  • contsupref The email number of the participant
  • password The password of the participant (unencrypted)
  • photo link The name of the photo of the user. Located in http://upstreamacademy.com/apps/...
  • memberships Comma separated field with associations or memberships (UAN,AGN,CPAmerica,etc.)
* Note: the case of the titles does not matter (ie: FIRST, FiRsT, and first are the same)
